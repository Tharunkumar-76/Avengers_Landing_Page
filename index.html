Red Hulk
General Thaddeus "Thunderbolt" Ross transforms into the Red Hulk, a beast of immense strength and heat generation, to hunt the Hulk.
Iron Man
Genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist. Tony Stark uses his high-tech armor to protect the world as the Invincible Iron Man.
Captain America
Steve Rogers, a super-soldier from World War II, leads the Avengers with his unwavering moral compass and vibranium shield.
Thor
The God of Thunder from Asgard, wielding his enchanted hammer Mjolnir, protects both the Nine Realms and Earth.
Spiderman
Peter Parker, a high school student bitten by a radioactive spider, fights crime in New York City as the friendly neighborhood Spider-Man.
Black Panther
T'Challa, the King of Wakanda, uses the power of the Black Panther and advanced vibranium technology to defend his nation and the world.
